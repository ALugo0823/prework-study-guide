# Prework Study Guide Webpage ## Description This webpage serves as a simple study guide for students enrolled in the coding bootcamp at the University of Arizona. This guide covers the content from the prework section of the course. Finally, the study guide covers the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, Git, and Javascript. ## Installation N/A ## Usage Each section of the prework curriculum contains notes that can be used to study. In addition, if the user needs a suggestion on what to study first, they can open the console by right clicking and selecting the "Inspect Element" option. When in the console, the panel will show the user what they should study first. ## Credits N/A ## License Please refer to the license in the repo.
